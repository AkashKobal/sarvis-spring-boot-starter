# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Sarvis Spring Boot Starter community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Acting professionally and respectfully

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful speech
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Threatening or abusive behavior
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional environment

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may take appropriate corrective action, including temporary or permanent bans, for behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories
- Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- Documentation
- Community forums
- Public or private communication related to the project

It also applies when an individual officially represents the project in public spaces, including social media, conferences, and other events.

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately.

GitHub Security Advisories:
https://github.com/AkashKobal/sarvis-spring-boot-starter/security/advisories

Maintainer Contact:
https://github.com/AkashKobal

Please include:

- Description of the incident
- Relevant links, screenshots, or logs
- Any additional context

All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these general enforcement guidelines:

### 1. Warning

A private warning explaining the violation and expected behavior.

### 2. Temporary Ban

Temporary restriction from contributing to the project.

### 3. Permanent Ban

Permanent removal from project participation.

Severity and enforcement actions will be determined at the discretion of the maintainers.
